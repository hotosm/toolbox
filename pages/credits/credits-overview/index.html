    This guide has been developed as part of the Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Project, funded by The World Bank’s Global Facility for Disaster Reduction and Recovery (GFDRR). This toolbox builds upon previous HOT projects including the Open Mapping for SDGs Guide and the Refugee Mapping Toolkit.
